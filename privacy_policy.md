# Privacy Policy — Voyage

**Last updated:** 31 October 2025
**Contact:** [info@voyageapp.pro](mailto:info@voyageapp.pro)

## 1) Who we are

Voyage (“we,” “us,” or “the App”) helps users practice **visa interviews** and **IELTS speaking** through interactive sessions, scoring, and coaching.

## 2) What this policy covers

This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, how we use and share it, and the choices you have. A privacy policy is required on Google Play and must clearly disclose collection, use, and sharing practices.

## 3) The data we collect

**Account & Profile**

* Email, name or display name (if provided)
* Country/region, visa category, study/work goals, and other profile fields you choose to add (e.g., program interest, funding plan)

**Practice Content**

* Your practice inputs and session artifacts (e.g., answers, transcripts, scores, feedback notes)
* **Audio/Video from practice sessions** when you choose microphone or camera features

**App & Device Info**

* Device model, OS version, app version, language, and basic diagnostics (crash logs, performance)
* Limited network data (e.g., IP at connection time) for security, fraud prevention, and regional compliance

We do **not** collect payment card numbers in the App.

## 4) Why we collect it (lawful purposes)

* Provide core features (simulate interviews, IELTS practice, generate feedback and progress)
* Improve reliability, quality, and safety (debugging, analytics, anti-abuse, service monitoring)
* Communicate important updates and support
* Comply with law and platform policies

Where required by law (e.g., the EU/EEA), we rely on one or more legal bases such as **consent**, **contract**, **legitimate interests**, or **legal obligation**, and we honor applicable data-subject rights.

## 5) How features use your device permissions

* **Microphone & Camera (optional):** used only for speaking practice, mock interviews, and generating performance feedback. Android treats these as sensitive permissions and expects clear disclosures.

You can disable these permissions in your OS settings; some features will not work without them.

## 6) How we use and share data

We use your data to run the App and to improve the experience. We **do not sell** your personal information and we **do not allow third-party ads** inside the App.

We share data only with contracted **service providers** who help us operate the App (e.g., hosting, authentication, analytics, content delivery, support). They may process data on our behalf under confidentiality and data-protection terms. Google Play requires accurate disclosure of collection and sharing in your listing’s **Data safety** section. 

If required by law or to protect users, we may disclose limited information to authorities.

## 7) Children

Voyage is intended for users **16+**. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under **13**. If we learn that a child under 13 has provided personal data, we will delete it. This aligns with COPPA obligations for online services directed to children or with actual knowledge of under-13 users.

## 8) International transfers

We may process and store information in countries outside your own. When we do, we use appropriate safeguards consistent with applicable law (e.g., contractual protections). Users in Nepal are protected by national privacy rules (Individual Privacy Act, 2018; Electronic Transactions Act) that reinforce consent and confidentiality of electronic data.

## 9) Data retention

* **Account & Profile:** kept while your account is active.
* **Practice Content (audio, video, transcripts, scores):** kept as long as needed to provide features like history, analytics, and progress, then deleted or de-identified.
  You can request deletion at any time (see Section 11). We aim to complete verified deletions within **30 days** unless a longer period is required by law or for safety.

## 10) Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect data (e.g., encryption in transit, access controls, and logging). No system is perfect, but we work to prevent unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. Google Play expects developers to disclose security practices in the **Data safety** form. 

## 11) Your choices and rights

**In-App Controls**

* Update profile info, manage microphone/camera use, review or clear session history (where available)
* Close your account by contacting us

**Legal rights (where applicable)**

* **GDPR (EU/EEA):** access, rectify, erase, restrict, data portability, object, and withdraw consent.
* **CCPA/CPRA (California):** know, delete, correct, opt-out of “sale”/“sharing,” and non-discrimination. We don’t “sell” personal information as defined by the law.
* **Nepal:** privacy in electronic data, use only for stated purposes and with consent. 

To exercise rights, email **[info@voyageapp.pro](mailto:info@voyageapp.pro)**. We may verify your request to protect your account.

## 12) Data about IELTS & interview practice

* **What’s stored:** session timestamps, question/answer content, transcripts, scores/metrics, and (if you enable mic/cam) audio/video from sessions.
* **What it’s for:** personalized coaching, progress tracking, and service quality.
* **What it’s not for:** third-party advertising or selling your personal data.

## 13) Changes to this policy

We will update this policy when we add features or when laws change. Where required, we will notify you in the App or by email before changes take effect.
